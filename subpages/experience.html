United States Coast Guard Sector New York

Cyber Security Intern

May 2023 - July 2022

One of the significant components of my research this summer was gathering diverse information about the cyber hygiene of ships. One of my mentors, Brendan Dolan, was able to capture the photo above while we were aboard a container ship. This information was collected to gain a better understanding of how cyber security is practiced in the maritime industry. The gathered data was utilized to develop a comprehensive cyber attack scenario targeting the USCG Sector New York and its area of responsibility. The primary focus of the cyber security exercises, scheduled for October 2023, is the facilities. However, it's also crucial for the Coast Guard and the other ports in the harbor to recognize that the safety of a port is only as strong as the security of the incoming vessels. My research and findings have contributed to a realistic cyber attack simulation that has led to or is leading to a successful cyber security exercise. Virtually the entire USCG Sector New York, along with the entire harbor area of New York up to Albany, NY, is benefiting from these efforts. In addition to the research conducted to build the mock cyber attack scenario, I have also developed a small website to promote cyber security awareness.
